# Ephemeral ✨
### *The moment is ephemeral. The creation is forever.*

An AI-powered fashion design assistant built for Indian and South Asian women — bridging the gap between imagination and the local tailor.

---

## The Problem

Millions of Indian women have a vision for their outfit but no way to communicate it. They visit tailors with vague descriptions, torn magazine pages, or nothing at all. The result? Disappointment.

Ephemeral gives that vision a voice.

---

## What Ephemeral Will Do

- **Imagination to Life** — describe your outfit in text or voice, get a generated design image
- **Tailor Brief Generator** — auto-generates a technical brief in tailor-friendly language, ready to share
- **Fabric Recommendation** — AI-guided fabric suggestions based on design, occasion, and budget
- **Try It On** — virtual try-on with front/back/side views
- **My Collection** — save and manage all your designs
- **Multilingual** — Hindi, English, and regional Indian languages
